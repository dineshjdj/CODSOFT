Features (Volatility-Based Stocks)

At Invest Wise, we categorize stocks based on their volatility characteristics to help investors make informed decisions. High Volatility Stocks experience significant price swings in short periods, while Medium Volatility Stocks exhibit moderate price movements. Low Volatility Stocks, on the other hand, are known for their stability and minimal price fluctuations.

High Volatility Stocks

Significant price fluctuations, higher risk.

Medium Volatility Stocks

Moderate price fluctuations, moderate risk.

Low Volatility Stocks

Stocks with relatively stable price movement.
